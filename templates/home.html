Mission Statement

There are many issues facing California right now and into the future. Some of the key areas I will focus on are education, crime, security, climate change, poverty and business. As you are all aware climate change is affecting all of us on a yearly basis and threatening our homes and livelihoods. It is extremely important that we tackle this early and fast. I will take the necessary steps to bring about fast action on this.

Education is another area that I am deeply passionate about. I believe it will benefit us all if we look after the future of our young people. Donations to school projects make up a large portion of school funding.
